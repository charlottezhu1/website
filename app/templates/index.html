I'm Charlotte, current MS student in Computer Science (HCI track) working in Stanford HCI group, advised by Prof. Michael Bernstein.

My research interests lie in designing and building interactive systems that understand and simulate human emotional experiences, to support human agency and enhance well-being in digital interactions.

During my undergraduate study at Stanford, I majored in Symbolic Systems (Human-Centered AI track) and East Asian Studies. I write and draw frequently in my free time.
